  • A Consistent Brand Identity

    With an award-winning 30 year history, The Boardroom's identity is paramount to its success. Learning about the history and intracies of the brand allow us to produce content that is in line with the target market, and looks pretty awesome too.

  • Print Isn't Dead

    We build stories that work across print and digital mediums. We ensure that every campaign delivers adequate attention to posters and billboards, as well as social media, e-mail newsletters, and online advertising campaigns.

  • Video Advertising

    Through the video advertising portion of our marketing plan, we are able to produce content that tells The Boardroom story. With targeted advertising, we ensure that the content reaches the right viewers.

  • Stay Core

    The Boardroom holds a special place in the hearts of Vancouverites. It's been a staple of the Kitsilano community for over 30 years. The shop got its start from skateboarders, and stays true to that foundation today.

  • Unique Visuals

    The opportunity to showcase great brands like Union Binding Co, Smith, Spy, Dragon, Vans, and over 200 more, allows us to tell a slightly different story with each campaign. This allows fluidity of the brand image and keeps The Boardroom looking fresh.
